RFM Analysis
Using SQL

The RFM model is a customer segmentation approach used to analyze customer profitability and maximize return on investment (ROI). By leveraging SQL, businesses can conduct RFM analysis to identify which customers are most valuable and prioritize efforts to retain them. This allows for targeted marketing strategies and personalized approaches to enhance customer satisfaction and increase profitability. RFM analysis enables businesses to efficiently process large volumes of customer data and gain valuable insights into customer behavior.
